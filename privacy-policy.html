Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 19, 2025

Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how Document Scanner & Manager ("we", "our", or "us") collects, uses, and protects your information when you use our mobile application (the "Service").

Information We Collect

Personal Information

  • User account information (if applicable)
  • Device information for app functionality

Document Data

  • Images of documents you scan using the app
  • Text extracted from documents via OCR technology
  • Document metadata (creation date, file size, etc.)

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide document scanning and OCR functionality
  • To organize and manage your documents within the app
  • To enable search and filtering capabilities
  • To improve app performance and user experience

Data Storage and Security

  • All documents and extracted text are stored locally on your device
  • We do not transmit your document data to external servers
  • We implement appropriate security measures to protect your data
  • You maintain full control over your document data

Permissions

Our app requires the following permissions:

  • Camera: To scan and capture document images
  • Storage: To save and access your documents on your device

Third-Party Services

Our app may use third-party libraries for:

  • OCR text extraction
  • Image processing
  • App analytics (anonymized data only)

Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

Data Retention

Your document data remains on your device until you choose to delete it. You can delete individual documents or all data through the app settings.

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your data stored in the app
  • Delete your documents and data at any time
  • Control app permissions through your device settings

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last updated" date.
